## 🌍 THE PROBLEM (Market Gap)

1. **Fast fashion dominates:** 90% of streetwear is trend-chasing, poor quality (120 GSM thin tees), and landfill-bound within 6 months.
2. **Hypebeast fatigue:** Consumers are tired of $200+ drops for mediocre quality + logos they don't relate to.
3. **Jacksonville gap:** No nationally recognized streetwear brand calls Jax home (Nike left in 2009, nothing filled the void).

---

## 💡 THE SOLUTION (Vibin Apparel)

**"Vibin Different."** — A brand posture, not just a tagline.

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|--------|
| **Heavyweight Cotton (240 GSM)** | Lasts 3x longer than fast fashion (50+ washes, no shrinkage) |
| **Minimal Design** | No loud logos, just intentional details (embroidered chest mark) |
| **Small-Batch Drops** | 500 units per design (creates scarcity, reduces inventory risk) |
| **Community-First** | Ambassadors earn 12% (not industry-standard 5-8%) |
| **Founder-Owned (Jax)** | Authentic Florida roots, not a VC-created "lifestyle" brand |
