## 💰 TAX OBLIGATIONS

### Federal (IRS)
- **LLC Taxation:** Single-member LLC = "disregarded entity" (Schedule C on personal return)
  - Consider S-Corp election (Form 2553) once net income >$60K/year
- **Self-Employment Tax:** 15.3% on net earnings (Social Security + Medicare)
- **Quarterly Estimates:** Due Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15, Jan 15
  - Calculate using Form 1040-ES (typically 25-30% of net income)

### State (Florida)
- ✅ No state personal income tax (Florida has 0% personal income tax!)
- ✅ Sales Tax: Collect 6% + local option tax (Duval County = 1%, total 7%)
  - File monthly/quarterly via Florida Department of Revenue
  - Use Stripe Tax to auto-calculate + remit

### Local (Jacksonville/Duval County)
- ✅ Business Tax Receipt: $50-100/year
- ✅ No city-specific sales tax (already covered by county)
